Software Design and Development :: CS 246

Week 04 :: Overview

Summary

This week you will learn about version control systems, how to use the git version control system, and how to use GitHub to collaborate with other developers.

Weekly Rhythm

Each week, you'll be expected to do the following:

  • Complete the Prepare assignment and quiz before class on Wednesday.

  • Face-to-Face students will complete the Teach assignment and quiz in class on Wednesday. Online students will complete the Teach assignment and quiz on their own.

  • Complete the Prove assignment and quiz by Saturday at midnight.

  • Complete the weekly self-assessment by Saturday at midnight.

In addition to these standard assignments, a major component of this course is the team project. Many weeks (including this one), will have a component of the team project that is due by Saturday at midnight.
